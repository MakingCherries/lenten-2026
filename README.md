# ✝ Lenten Journey 2026

A devotional web application for Lent 2026 (Ash Wednesday, February 18 – Easter Sunday, April 5).

## Features

* **Daily Mass Readings (NABRE)** — First Reading, Responsorial Psalm, Second Reading (Sundays/Solemnities), and Holy Gospel — all 47 days hardcoded, no API needed
* **Saint of the Day** — Biography and feast day information
* **Daily Meditation** — 40 unique quotes from saints, Scripture, and theologians
* **Prayer of the Day** — Rotating traditional Catholic prayers
* **Fasting & Abstinence Reminders** — Auto-detected on Ash Wednesday and Fridays
* **Stations of the Cross** — All 14 stations with reflection text and prayer
* **Lenten Progress Tracker** — Visual progress bar from Ash Wednesday to Easter
* **Day Navigation** — Browse any day of Lent forward or back
* **Auto-detects Today's Date** — Opens to the current day of Lent automatically

## Special Liturgies

* **Palm Sunday** — Procession Gospel (Matthew 21) + Mass Readings including the Passion (Matthew 26–27)
* **Holy Thursday** — Evening Mass of the Lord's Supper
* **Good Friday** — Passion of the Lord (John 18–19)
* **Easter Vigil** — All 7 Old Testament Readings with Responsorial Psalms, Epistle (Romans 6), and Gospel (Matthew 28)
* **Easter Sunday** — Mass of Easter Day

## Readings

All readings are from the **New American Bible, Revised Edition (NABRE)**, Year A Sunday cycle. Text is hardcoded — no external API calls, works completely offline.

## Tech Stack

* Vanilla HTML, CSS, JavaScript — no dependencies, no build step
* Google Fonts: Cormorant Garamond, Cinzel, Crimson Text
* Mobile responsive
